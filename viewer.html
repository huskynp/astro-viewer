The Andromeda Galaxy, also known as Messier 31, is a neighbor galaxy of the Milky Way. The galaxy was discovered more than a thousand years ago, and it has over a trillion stars. It is on a collision course with our Milky Way, and the two galaxies are expected to collide in around 5 billion years.

Distance to Earth: 2.5 million light years

Type: Barred Spiral Galaxy
