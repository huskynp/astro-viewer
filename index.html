astroviewer - A telescope in your phone

Explore the Stars

Controls: Move your phone around to explore. Pinch to zoom and rotate. Works best on a phone!
Uses the Aladin API, and data from various sky surveys.



Space is pretty huge. And pretty pretty. There's tons of amazing objects to look at, from our neighbor galaxies to oddly-shaped nebulas. But it's impossible at home to appreciate the beauty of the stars and galaxies near us (especially in light-polluted cities). This site allows you to gaze the sky from the perspective of the most advanced telescopes and sky surveys humanity has ever created.
